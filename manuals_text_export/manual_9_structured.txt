# Operating Instructions TechniSat DVR-App for iPad, iPhone, iPod touch

## Software Installation

1. First, download the “DVR (PVR)” app from the iTunes AppStore and wait until the automatic installation is complete.

*Note:* The app can be found in the iTunes AppStore under the "Utilities" category.
Alternatively, you can download the DVR (PVR) app using the following link: http://itunes.apple.com/de/app/dvr-pvr/id387948823?mt=8

## Usage

1. Start the app from the home screen of your iPad, iPhone or iPod touch.

*Note:* The login screen appears.
